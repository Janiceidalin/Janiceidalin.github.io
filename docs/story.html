Taiwanese Handmade Ceramic Teapot

My dad, LIN SUNG PEN 林松本 is a ceramist.

He can make any kind of potterys such as bowls, plates, vases, and especially teapots.

His teapots are very different from the ordianry ones which we used to see in our daily life.

Some might regard his teapots as arts, instead of teaopot.

Because they actually don't look like teapot but other things.

For example, pumpkins, bamboo, wood, or even turtles.

They aren't made by potter's wheel or models

He made them by his own hands.
