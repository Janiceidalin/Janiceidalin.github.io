Taiwanese Handmade Ceramic Teapot


My dad, LIN SUNG PEN 林松本 is a CERAMIST.

I used to call him, THE GREATEST CERAMIST.(LOL)

He can make any kinds of potterys such as bowls, plates, vases, and especially teapots.

His teapots are very different from the ordinary ones which we used to see in our daily life.

Some might regard them as arts, instead of teapots.

Because they actually don't look like teapots but other things.

For example , pumpkins , bamboo , wood , or even turtles .

He's good at mimicing various creatures and plants throughout the nature.

The creations look so real that most of the people can't believe they were actually made of clay.

And that's how special and wonderful his teapots are !

What's more,

The teapots aren't made by potter's wheel or models

Instead,

He made them by his own hands!!!

You probably think that it's not something really cool.

However...

It's never easy to shape the body into a very round ball without machine or models!

If you still don't believe it...
