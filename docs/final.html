TEAPOTAMI

Do you like to drink tea ? Do you brew tea for yourself? Or you always buy a bottle of tea from 7-11 ? Maybe you know what a teapot look like. However, our teapots you will absolutely stun you ! They are ordinary or for old people Instead, they are SPECIAL!!! Want to learn more? Read our story and watch our videos with the link above!!


My dad, LIN SUNG PEN 林松本 is a CERAMIST. I used to call him, THE GREATEST CERAMIST.(LOL) He can make any kinds of potterys such as bowls, plates, vases, and especially teapots. His teapots are very different from the ordinary ones which we used to see in our daily life. Some might regard them as arts, instead of teapots. Because they actually don't look like teapots but other things. For example , pumpkins , bamboo , wood , or even turtles . He's good at mimicing various creatures and plants throughout the nature. The creations look so real that most of the people can't believe they were actually made of clay. And that's how special and wonderful his teapots are ! What's more, The teapots aren't made by potter's wheel or models Instead, He made them by his own hands !!! You probably think that it's not something really cool. However... It's never easy to shape the body into a very round ball without machine or models! If you still don't believe it... WELCOME TO OUR STUDIO AND TRY IT YOURSELF! And I'm sure that you will realize the difficulty right away! (EVIL SMILE...)
